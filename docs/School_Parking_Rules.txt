School Parking Rules

1. Student Parking Permit: All students must obtain a parking permit from the administration office before parking on campus. Permits must be displayed clearly on the rearview mirror.
2. Designated Areas: Students may only park in the designated student parking lot. Staff and visitor lots are off-limits.
3. Speed Limit: The campus speed limit is 10 mph at all times.
4. No Idling: Engines must be turned off when parked. No idling is allowed on school grounds.
5. Violations: Any unauthorized parking, double parking, or blocking driveways will result in a citation or towing at the owner’s expense.
6. Security: The school is not responsible for theft or damage to vehicles. Lock your doors and keep valuables out of sight.
7. Suspension of Privileges: Repeated violations may lead to loss of parking privileges for the semester.
