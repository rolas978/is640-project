Attendance Rules

1. Daily Attendance: Students are expected to attend all classes on time every day.
2. Excused Absences: Absences due to illness, family emergencies, or school-approved activities must be reported by a parent or guardian within 24 hours.
3. Tardiness: Students arriving after the bell are considered tardy. Three tardies equal one unexcused absence.
4. Early Dismissal: Students must have written permission from a parent or guardian and approval from the office before leaving early.
5. Make-Up Work: It is the student’s responsibility to request and complete missed assignments within three days of returning to class.
6. Excessive Absences: More than 10 unexcused absences per semester may result in a failing grade or disciplinary action.
